Common Questions

Quick answers

Do I need an appointment?
Appointments are strongly recommended to guarantee your preferred stylist and time slot. We do accept walk-ins based on availability — give us a call before stopping by!
How do I book with a specific stylist?
Just call us at (352) 620-9360 and let us know which team member you'd like to book with. You can also browse our staff page to find the right match.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, all major credit and debit cards. Gift certificates are also available and can be purchased in any amount — perfect for a thoughtful gift!
Do you offer bridal packages?
Absolutely! We assemble our full team for bridal parties — hair, nails, and makeup for the bride and her entire party. Contact us early as bridal dates book up fast.
Where exactly are you located?
We're at 104 SE 1st Ave, Suite D in beautiful Downtown Ocala, FL 34471. There is parking available nearby. Call us if you need help finding us!
Do you have monthly specials?
Yes! We rotate specials every month. Check our Specials page regularly, and follow us on social media so you never miss a deal or a new gift certificate offer.
